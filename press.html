Author bio

Sage Halcyon

Sage Halcyon writes cozy science fiction romance for readers who want the wonder of space without the dread: ships with herb gardens instead of weapons bays, crews who take care of each other, and love stories that unfold slowly enough to enjoy the view.

Her Thistledown series follows a small domed settlement at the edge of nowhere, where every book is its own complete romance and the town itself keeps growing around them, one repair, seedling, coffee refill, and soft landing at a time.

Short Bio

Sage Halcyon writes cozy sci-fi romance about greenhouse domes, found family, practical tenderness, and people learning that staying can be its own kind of bravery.

Series Summary

Thistledown is a cozy sci-fi romance series set in a small domed settlement where every book follows a different couple and a complete love story. Expect low-dread science fiction, slow trust, found family, gentle humor, practical caretaking, and endings that leave the lights on.
