About Sage

Hello from the greenhouse deck.

Sage Halcyon writes science fiction romance for readers who want the wonder of space without the dread: ships with herb gardens instead of weapons bays, crews who take care of each other, and love stories that unfold slowly enough to actually enjoy the view.

The Thistledown books orbit a small domed settlement where every romance stands on its own, but the town keeps growing around them, one repair, seedling, and soft landing at a time.
